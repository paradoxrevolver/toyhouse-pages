About

Aethiss is a gray-furred Camerack catdragon. Her short fur sports a blue tinge and in the right light you could swear she was colored pure white. She's an avid reader and a perky, enthuastic adventurer.

Throughout her childhood and observation of human families she grew a deep longing for her parents, although she has never been able to find them. As a result, her goal in life is to experience everything and become a part of as many people's lives as possible. She spends most of her time reading about the culture and languages of other species on Tisck and occasionally makes trips long distances to learn about them in person, but she always returns home to her friends to attend to her most important relationships.

Aethiss never ties up her hair unless it's absolutely necessary. She types in all lowercase because she believes that it makes the moments when she uses uppercase that much more impactful. She can speak in a wide variety of languages, although she's really only mastered the Camerack's native language and the most common language between all species.
