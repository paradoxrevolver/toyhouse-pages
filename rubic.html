About

Rubic is a black-furred Camerack catdragon. His fur is surprisingly short but his scales underneath are an identical color that makes it impossible to tell its true length. He has a number of whiskers at the base of his jaw, which are not terribly common among Camerack, but are so thin that they generally go unnoticed.

Rubic is shy but friendly to anyone he gets to know well. He spends most of his time indoors, tinkering with gadgets and computers. An optimist and problem-solver at heart, he seeks challenges whether they're logical or emotional and is always willing to help someone in need.

His favorite color lies somewhere between green and blue. He almost exclusively wears cool colors but is much quicker to wear anything with an obscure and abstract design on it, even if it's meaningless. He types in all lowercase and completely avoids uppercase letters altogether, but he never forgets punctuation.
